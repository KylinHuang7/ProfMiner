# 🤍 Opal Series

Opal is a gorgeous decorative material similar to quartz, with a milky white appearance and elegant patterns.

## Opal Ore

![Opal Ore](../screenshots/opal/opal_ore.png)

- **Generation Range**: Y = -40 ~ 40, uniform distribution
- **Vein Size**: Up to 32 blocks per vein, 5 attempts per chunk
- **Mining Requirement**: Stone Pickaxe or above
- **Drops**: 3~6 Opals (supports Fortune enchantment; Silk Touch drops the ore block itself)
- **No experience drops**
