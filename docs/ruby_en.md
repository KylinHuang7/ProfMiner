# 💎 Ruby Series

## Ruby Ore

![Ruby Ore](../screenshots/ruby/ruby_ore.png)

- **Generation Range**: Y = -59 ~ -10, uniform distribution
- **Vein Size**: Up to 4 blocks per vein, 1 attempt per chunk
- **Mining Requirement**: Iron Pickaxe or above
- **Drops**: Ruby (supports Fortune enchantment; Silk Touch drops the ore block itself)
- **Experience Drops**: 3~7 points
- **Variants**: Generates in both Stone and Deepslate variants (Deepslate Ruby Ore)

## Ruby

![Ruby](../screenshots/ruby/ruby.png)

- Dropped from Ruby Ore, used to craft Ruby Heart

## Ruby Heart

- **Crafting Recipe**:
![Ruby Heart Crafting](../screenshots/ruby/ruby_heart.png)

- **Function**: Right-click to use, **permanently increases max health by 2 points** (1 heart)
![Ruby Heart Usage](../screenshots/ruby/ruby_heart2.png)

- **Maximum**: Can increase from 10 hearts up to 20 hearts (40 health points)
- **Stack Size**: Up to 16
- **Usage Effect**: Plays a level-up sound and immediately restores the newly added health
